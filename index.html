02 — CURRENT STATION

Remote Ham Radio

📡 RemoteHamRadio (RHR)

I am also using RemoteHamRadio (RHR) to expand my operating experience and improve my skills on HF.

Through RHR, I have access to high-performance remote stations located in low-noise environments, allowing me to:

  • Operate on multiple HF bands under excellent conditions
  • Improve my pile-up handling and DX operating skills
  • Gain experience with different station setups and antennas
  • Make contacts that would be difficult from my home QTH

This is a great complement to my personal station and helps me progress faster in the hobby.
